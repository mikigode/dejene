Amol Cable - Backend Develpoer

Website providing electrical equipment with product authenticity system

Amol Cable

The AmolCable website has become one of the best websites in its field with the production of electrical equipment and the launch of the product authenticity check system. This company has been able to produce quality products based on international standards. The website of this company provides complete information about its products.

One of the main challenges in this project has been to set up a product authenticity check system. Considering the high number of products and the demand of customers to ensure the authenticity of the product, the launch of such a system has been of great importance.

In order to implement the product authenticity check system on the AmolCable website, I was looking for a solution that would assure customers of the authenticity of the company's products. One of the main challenges of this program was finding a way to verify the authenticity of the high volume items.

To solve this challenge, I first wrote a program to generate barcodes for each item. Then this barcode code was integrated in all packages. Then, using a portable barcode scanner, the product's authenticity was checked. By scanning the barcode, this device sent the product information to the server, and the server used its own algorithms to verify the authenticity of the product.

This solution made it easy and fast to check the authenticity of the product, and in this way, customers can easily distinguish original products from fake ones.

By implementing this system, AmolCable can have more trust in its customers and thus improve the number of customers and its market share. Overall, AmolCable website is a prime example in this field
