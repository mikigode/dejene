Proganic Market

Proganic Market website and application is an online store for organic products that allows customers to buy healthy and organic products online. This platform provides users with complete confidence and satisfaction by providing quality and organic products.

Online stores always face various technical challenges. In the case of Proganic Market, there have been challenges that must be considered for the successful implementation of this store. Some of the technical challenges of this store are:

1. Product management: Considering that Proganic Market operates as an organic store, it must be able to manage a high volume of organic products such as fruits, vegetables, grain products and other various organic products.

2. Search and filtering: Despite the wide variety of products, users need to find the products they want quickly and accurately. Therefore, implementing an optimal and efficient search and filtering system is one of the challenges of this store.

3. Shopping cart management and payment: Another important challenge of online stores is shopping cart management and the payment process. Facilities such as adding and removing products from the shopping cart, calculating the final price should be implemented

Finally, this website was able to respond to the different needs of its customers by using various facilities.
